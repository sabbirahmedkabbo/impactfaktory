Nexon Beverages

Hijack the Grid

Marketing

Challenge

Nexon — a new energy drink — needed to break into a crowded category dominated by two legacy brands with ten-figure budgets.

Solution

A city-wide OOH takeover combined with an underground influencer drop campaign disguised as a scavenger hunt — generating earned media before a single paid placement ran.

Result

+340% brand search volume in 6 weeks.
2.7M social impressions at launch.
Stocked in 1,200 new retail locations.

Solaris Music Festival

The Living Brand

Event Activation

Challenge

A mid-tier music festival needed to transform its brand from generic event venue to cultural destination — without doubling its headline budget.

Solution

End-to-end experiential design: immersive brand zones, custom AR installations, and a crowd-sourced art installation that became the festival's social centrepiece.

Result

+68% ticket sales YoY.
4.1M impressions from festival UGC.
Covered by 14 editorial outlets.

Volta Financial

Authority by Design

Brand Identity

Challenge

A fintech startup with strong fundamentals was indistinguishable from a hundred competitors. Series A was stalling because investors didn't believe the brand.

Solution

Complete rebrand: stripped the visual system, rebuilt from positioning-first logic, created a new wordmark system, typographic hierarchy, and investor-specific narrative package.

Result

Series A closed 3 weeks post-rebrand.
+190% pitch-to-meeting conversion.
Used as case study at two fintech conferences.

Arcos Construction

The Comeback Architecture

Perception Shift

Challenge

A regional contractor faced a PR crisis after a disputed project attracted negative media coverage. New business pipeline dropped 45% in one quarter.

Solution

18-month narrative engineering programme: reframed company history, deployed community-anchored storytelling, engineered social proof, and coordinated editorial placement in trade press.

Result

Pipeline recovered to 115% of pre-crisis levels.
Net Promoter Score +34 points.
Three unsolicited award nominations.
